Welcome to Chairman

You’re the owner of a club in the English pyramid. There is no “match engine” to click each minute — time moves in weeks. Your job is the balance sheet, the squad, and the story you build.

What you’re aiming for

  • Climb the leagues — promotion and cups raise reputation and unlock better money.
  • Stay solvent — wages, stadium upkeep, transfers and youth spend all hit cash; TV and gates bring it back.
  • Keep stakeholders onside — fans, investors, and media react to results and your decisions.
  • Grow off the pitch — sponsors, staff, satellite clubs, and franchise deals stack over seasons.

How a typical week works

  1. Click Advance week — finances tick (wages, TV, debt interest, etc.).
  2. In season, you get league and cup results (cups can fall on separate weeks from league games).
  3. Use Transfers to sign players on contracts; check Inbox for staff and board events.
  4. Between seasons: off-season then pre-season weeks before fixtures resume.

How to play

Chairman is a long-form owner sim: advance time week by week, manage money and people, and climb from the lower leagues.

Core loop

  • Advance week runs the economy and (in season) fixtures.
  • Watch cash, debt, wages on the bar — crisis loans and naming rights exist if you’re stretched.
  • Finances shows a weekly P&L and season one-offs (transfers, youth, stadium build).

Football side

  • Squad / Transfers — sign players on contracts; other clubs may bid for yours.
  • Pyramid — scout and negotiate with other clubs’ players.
  • Cups run on separate calendar steps from league games.

Between seasons

After the league ends you get off-season and pre-season weeks (no league table pressure) before the next campaign.

Club inbox

Staff notes and board decisions land here — same as club email.
